Mandarin Tones

Mandarin Chinese is a tonal language with four main tones and a neutral tone. The meaning of a word changes depending on the tone used.

1st Tone
High and level tone
妈
mā (mother)
2nd Tone
Rising tone
麻
má (hemp)
3rd Tone
Falling-rising tone
马
mǎ (horse)
4th Tone
Falling tone
骂
mà (scold)
