English Grammar Essentials

English grammar is the set of structural rules that govern the composition of clauses, phrases, and words in the English language. Here are some essential grammar points to master:

Tense Structure Example
Present Simple Subject + V1 (+ s/es for 3rd person) I work. She works.
Present Continuous Subject + am/is/are + V-ing I am working. She is working.
Past Simple Subject + V2 (past form) I worked. She worked.
Past Continuous Subject + was/were + V-ing I was working. They were working.
Present Perfect Subject + have/has + V3 (past participle) I have worked. She has worked.
Future Simple Subject + will + V1 I will work. She will work.
