Art & Literature

France has a rich artistic heritage, from the Impressionist paintings of Monet and Renoir to the literary works of Victor Hugo and Albert Camus. The Louvre in Paris is the world's most visited museum.

Etiquette & Customs

French social etiquette includes greeting with "la bise" (cheek kisses), using formal "vous" versus informal "tu", and maintaining proper table manners. Politeness is highly valued in French society.
