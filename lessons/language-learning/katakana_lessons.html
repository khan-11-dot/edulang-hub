When to Use Katakana

Foreign Words

Used for words borrowed from other languages, like コーヒー (koohii - coffee) and パン (pan - bread from Portuguese "pão")

Foreign Names

For non-Japanese names, like マイケル (Maikeru - Michael) and エマ (Ema - Emma)

Onomatopoeia

For sound effects, like ドキドキ (dokidoki - heartbeat) and ワンワン (wanwan - dog barking)
